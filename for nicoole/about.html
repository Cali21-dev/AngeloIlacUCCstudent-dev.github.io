About CE

The College of Education is an academic institution where students train to become teachers or professional educators. It focuses on imparting knowledge, skills, and values to inspire future generations and promote quality education, academic excellence, and student development, positively impacting lives. The College provides a rich array of programs and courses aimed at equipping students for successful careers in education and allied fields. These programs encompass a broad range of educational disciplines, including elementary and secondary education, special education, counseling, and educational leadership. With a focus on both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, the college prepares future educators and professionals to meet the diverse needs of learners and contribute to the advancement of the education system.

Opportunity

The College of Education at DMMMSU MLUC offers distinguished opportunities for future educators through comprehensive in-service programs, hands-on training, and access to laboratory schools. These programs enable students to develop essential teaching skills, gain practical experience, and contribute meaningfully to advancements in educational practices and innovation.

Passing Rate

The overall performance of the College of Education in the March 2024 Licensure Examination for Teachers reflects our commitment to excellence.
