Our Educational Programs

Bachelor of Education Programs

Our College of Education offers a variety of undergraduate programs designed to prepare future educators. We focus on delivering quality education through modern teaching methodologies, interactive learning, and practical teaching experience.

We offer the following programs:

  • Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEED)
  • Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSED) in different majors
  • Bachelor of Physical Education (BPEd)
  • Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teacher Education (BTVTED)

Each program is crafted to ensure that students are well-equipped to excel in the classroom and make a meaningful impact in the education sector.
